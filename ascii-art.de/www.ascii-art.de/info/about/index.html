The Author

The Ascii Art Dictionary was created by Andreas Freise. The idea, the design, the coding, - you name it, all that is my own responsibility. And of course I am the sole maintainer too. On average I spend half an hour per day on this manifestation of my ASCII addiction.

The ASCII addiction also includes the making of ASCII pictures. You can find most of my pictures in my Gallery and some more information about the `artist' in me by clicking on my initials in any of my ASCII drawings.

Maintenance is a task that gets simpler and gives better results when the user, i.e. you, takes part by giving feedback. So, please correct me when I am wrong. Please send suggestions, criticism or your emotional state while watching my pages. And of course I do take submissions of ASCII pictures!

Feedback? Where to? Please drop me a mail at:

andreas@ascii-art.de
